About Us

BSIT 21002 Parking System

The website features a responsive design with a centered navigation bar, links to key sections, and a mobile-friendly menu. The Home page includes a hero section, services showcase, online booking, and contact information, with a footer displaying the Bestlink College logo. The Attendant View provides a dedicated interface, while the Booking system allows easy slot reservations with detailed information and options for VIPs. A Dashboard offers monitoring and control of slots, occupancy, and revenue. The Login/Registration form gathers essential user details. Developed using HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL, and JavaScript on XAMPP, the system aims to streamline parking, enhance convenience, and provide school benefits by reducing parking time and increasing security. Future researchers may expand on its impact on space utilization.
